Instructions:

1. Go to your playlist's page (using firefox or chrome).
2. Make sure to have all videos loaded (clicking on "load more" button) if you want all videos randomized.
3. Switch your playlist settings ordering to manual if it's not there.
4. Open Console (ctrl+shift+i).
5. Copy/paste the content of randomize.js on console and press enter.
6. After script is done, refresh the page.
7. Good luck!
